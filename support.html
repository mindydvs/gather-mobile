Gather Support

We're here to help.

Gather is a shared group calendar that keeps your family, friends, and teammates on the same page. If you need help, have a question, or want to report a problem, this page has you covered.

Getting Started

  • Create an account with your name and email to get started.
  • Create a group for your family, team, or any crew you want to share plans with.
  • Add events by tapping a date and filling in the details.
  • Invite people by email, or share a join link — no app download required for them to view the invite.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite someone to my group?

Open your group, tap the invite icon, and either enter their email address or share the group's join link.

How do I switch between groups?

Tap the group name at the top of the calendar to switch between all the groups you belong to.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password" and we'll email you a reset link.

How do I leave a group?

Open the group's member list and choose to leave the group. This removes you from that group's shared calendar.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at the address below and we'll permanently delete your account and all associated data.
